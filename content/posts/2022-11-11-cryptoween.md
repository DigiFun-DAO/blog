You are welcome to join us on one of the greatest Halloween parties in the metaverse!

On October 30th, 2022, DigiFun brought Halloween to the metaverse with a Cryptoween party in collaboration with CKBubbles, a creative director and nail artist  in Decentraland! This was the first Halloween-themed event hosted by DigiFun and it was definitely one of the most successful parties.

Cryptoween was an immersive party where you can see little dinosaurs, vampires, astronauts, skeleton angels and more in rather creative costumes. DigiFun also had a friendly contest to award the "Most Creative Costume" with a set of the latest Halloween-themed wearables collaborated with CKBubbles.

AI Glamora dress was launched on Halloween weekend in Decentraland leading up to the MVMF (Metaverse Music Festival)  where Cyberpunk meets nature and music. The dress was designed by CKBubbles using mid journey and open AI to test and use for sketches, using the prompts "sheer pink iris van herpen sci fi fantasy dress"...
then provided to DigiFun to model.
