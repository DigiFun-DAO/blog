In September 2022, following 'Pride Phoenix Wings', DigiFun created a second wearable collection with designer Morchen.

Inspired by Chinese white and blue porcelain. Decorated by hand with blue pigment, which has been produced for over 1,000 years.

The red liquid forms from the vase, slowly dripping down to reveal the snake dress.  Red snakes intertwine and form a complex armour, which is engraved with Chinese traditional auspicious clouds and dragons.
